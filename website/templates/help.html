What is the WhoDat?

The WhoDat is a centralized database and user interface designed to facilitate the management of personnel movements within the company. When an individual undergoes a role change or plans to leave the company, they can access this platform to review their current roles and responsibilities. With a single button click, they can easily notify the relevant individuals about their impending changes.

Tips and advice

You can conduct searches directly from the home page. Enter either a complete 6-digit staff number or a full or partial first or second name. The tool will generate a list of matches based on your search query.

To notify the process owner, simply click the 'Inform' button located at the end of each row.

Click on your profile picture to edit your account.
